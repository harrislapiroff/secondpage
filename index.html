About the Second Page

The Second Page is a storytelling radio show hosted by Harris Lapiroff and Seán Hanson. Each week, we share true stories by actual Second Page listeners, themed around a particular topic.

The first season of The Second Page aired on WOBC weekly from January through April of 2013.

The second season is airing right here, right now.
