Story Guidelines

See below for upcoming themes.

  • Anyone may submit a story!
  • Interpret the theme as strictly or loosely as you want.
  • Attach a brief written bio, along the lines of “Harris Lapiroff is an Oberlin College alumn from the class of 2011. He makes websites and art in Oberlin, Ohio.”
  • Aim for a story of 5 to 15 minutes. If it needs to be shorter, don't pad it. If it’s really fascinating, go ahead and make it longer.
  • Record in a quiet location, to the extent available. If possible use an external microphone to avoid getting computer fan noise.
  • Try to follow FCC regulations with respect to obscenity, indecency, and profanity.
  • By submitting a story, you agree to allow me to release it with the rest of the episode under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike license.
